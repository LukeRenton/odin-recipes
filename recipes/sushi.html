Sushi Recipe

Description

Sushi is a Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice, usually with some sugar and salt, accompanying a variety of ingredients, such as seafood, vegetables, and occasionally tropical fruits. Styles of sushi and its presentation vary widely, but the one key ingredient is "sushi rice", also referred to as shari, or sumeshi.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • Nori seaweed sheets
  • Fish of your choice
  • Vegetables of your choice
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi
  • Pickled ginger

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice
  2. Cook the rice
  3. Prepare the rice vinegar
  4. Spread the rice
  5. Prepare the fish and vegetables
  6. Roll the sushi
  7. Cut the sushi
  8. Chow
